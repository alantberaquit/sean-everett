Science Study Guide

Grade 6 • Quarter 3 • Keywords Only

BIG IDEA

Vertebrates → WITH backbone Invertebrates → NO backbone Backbone = Spine

VERTEBRATES (5 GROUPS)

Fish
Water Gills Fins Scales
Amphibians
Land + Water Moist skin Change form Frog
Reptiles
Land Dry scales Lungs Snake
Birds
Feathers Wings Eggs Chicken
Mammals
Fur / Hair Milk Live birth Human

INVERTEBRATES

Arthropods
Jointed legs Exoskeleton Ant Spider Crab
Mollusks
Soft body Shell Snail
Annelids
Segmented body Earthworm
Echinoderms
Spiny skin Sea star
Cnidarians
Stinging cells Jellyfish

EXAM REMINDERS

With spine → Vertebrate No spine → Invertebrate 6 legs → Insect 8 legs → Arachnid Shell + soft → Mollusk Segmented worm → Annelid

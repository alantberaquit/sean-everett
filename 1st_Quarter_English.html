Test II – Essay (Items 21–30)
Directions: Read the passage for Items 21–25, then write a 3–5 sentence summary using your own words. For Items 26–30, write a 3–5 sentence paragraph connecting information to your personal experience.
Passage for Summarizing (Items 21–25)
“The mango tree is one of the most popular fruit trees in the Philippines. It grows well in tropical weather and provides sweet, juicy fruits. Mangoes are eaten fresh or made into juices, jams, and dried snacks. Because of its popularity, the mango is considered the national fruit of the Philippines.”
21–25) Write a short summary of the passage. Keep it shorter than the text and focus on the main idea with only key details.
26–30) Making Connections: Think of a time you learned something new (from a book, video, or lesson). Explain in 3–5 sentences how you connected this information to your personal life or experiences.

Hint: Use connectors like “This reminds me of…,” “I experienced…,” “In my life…”

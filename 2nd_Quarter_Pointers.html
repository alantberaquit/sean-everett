CLVE (Christian Living Values Education)

Topics:

  • Faith and Praying
    • The Mysteries of the Holy Rosary: Joyful, Sorrowful, Glorious, Luminous
  • The First Commandment
  • The Second Commandment

Stories to Review:

  • God tests Abraham’s Faith (Genesis 22:1–18)
  • St. Lorenzo Ruiz’s Life
  • Worship the Lord your God! (Matthew 4:1–11)
  • Exodus 3:1–17

Test Format:

  • Multiple Choice (15 pts)
  • Identification – The Mysteries (10 pts)
  • True or False (15 pts)

➡️ Total: 40 points

AP (Araling Panlipunan)

Topics:

  • Mga Pagbabago sa Pamahalaan at Ekonomiya sa Panahon ng mga Amerikano
    • Pamahalaang Militar, Komisyon Schurman, Komisyon Taft
    • Philippine Bill (1902)
    • Philippine Assembly (1907)
    • Batas Jones (1916)
    • Unang Misyong Pangkalayaan ni Quezon (1919)
    • Misyong OsRox (1931)
    • Batas Hare-Hawes Cutting (1932)
    • Ikalawang Misyong Pangkalayaan ni Quezon (1933)
    • Batas Tydings-McDuffie (1934)
    • Constitutional Convention / Pagbuo ng Saligang Batas (1935)
  • Pamumuhay ng mga Pilipino noong Panahon ng Komonwelt
    • Layunin ng Pamahalaang Komonwelt
    • Batas Tanggulan Pambansa (Commonwealth Act No. 1)
    • Wikang Pambansa (Tagalog → Filipino)
    • Kahalagahan ng kababaihan (karapatang bumoto at manungkulan)
    • Pag-unlad sa Mindanao (Homestead Program)
    • NEPA – National Economic Protectionism Association (“Buy Philippines”)

Test Format:

  • Multiple Choice
  • Arrange the Sequence (Timeline)
  • Explanation

Science

Topics:

  • Musculoskeletal System
    • Functions: movement, support, protection
    • Major parts: bones, muscles, joints, cartilage, ligaments, tendons
  • Integumentary System
    • Functions: protection, regulation of body temp, sense of touch
    • Major parts: skin (epidermis, dermis), hair, nails, sweat & oil glands

Test Format:

  • Fill in the Blank
  • True or False
  • Case Study
  • Concept Mapping
Tip for Sean
  • For CLVE → memorize the Mysteries & know key Bible story details.
  • For AP → review the timeline of laws, missions, and government changes.
  • For Science → focus on functions & parts of each system (make mini flashcards).
